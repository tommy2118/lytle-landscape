Meet Steve Lytle

Steve Lytle is the heart of Lytle Landscape Construction. As a sole proprietor, certified horticulturist, and California Certified Nursery Professional (CCNP) with over 35 years of experience, Steve takes pride in bringing his clients' visions to life, one personalized landscape at a time. His hands-on approach ensures that each project receives the care and expertise it deserves, from initial consultation to final installation.

Steve’s deep understanding of plants and horticultural principles, combined with his CCNP certification, allows him to design landscapes that thrive in the unique climate of Orange County. His work combines artistic vision with a solid foundation in sustainable, practical landscaping.
