Our Story

Founded over 35 years ago, Lytle Landscape Construction has grown from a small, local landscaping business into a trusted name in Orange County. Our founder, Steve Lytle, started with a vision: to combine creativity, horticultural expertise, and exceptional service to deliver beautiful and functional landscapes. Today, we remain dedicated to bringing that vision to life with every project.
